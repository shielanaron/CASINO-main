How do the bonus rounds or jackpot features work?

Bonus rounds and jackpot features vary by game type. In slot machines, they are usually triggered by special symbols like scatters and can include free spins, pick-a-prize games, or multiplier rounds; jackpots may be fixed or progressive, increasing as more players participate. Matching games may offer power-ups or hidden bonus levels that activate after achieving specific goals, like clearing all cards within a limited number of moves. In Plinko or Clinco games, bonus features can be triggered when the chip lands in a special zone or activates a multiplier slot.

Which games offer the highest payouts?

Games that offer the highest payouts include progressive slot machines like Mega Moolah or Divine Fortune, where jackpots grow with every player’s bet and can reach massive amounts. High-volatility slot games also have the potential for very large payouts, although wins are less frequent. Plinko games can deliver high maximum rewards—sometimes up to 1,000 times your bet—but landing the top prize is rare. Some matching games provide smaller, more consistent wins due to their lower volatility.
